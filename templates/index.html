And finally, do you want to lease your car or finance it? Financing your car is making a monthly payment to buy your car. Basically, the money you spent goes to owning the car, so it’s technically free! Leasing is like a rental where you make monthly payments to use a vehicle for a set period of time instead of buying it. You can pay small amounts for a car to test it out, so it’s basically a free trial of a car!
